Pan-Seared Steak

Description

This delicious steak is quick and easy to cook, with minimal prep and cleanup.

Ingredients

  • 1x Steak (Ribeye or NY Strip Recommended)
  • 1x Clove of Garlic, Minced
  • 1/4 Stick Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 Handful of Thyme
  • 1 Pinch of Salt, Coursely Ground
  • 1 Pinch of Pepper, Coursely Ground

Steps

  1. Remove steak from fridge 30 minutes before cooking. This will enusre steak is room temperature and cooks evenly.
  2. Season steak on both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. When 30 minutes have passed, pour olive oil in pan and set to high heat. Wait for oil to get hot, when the first wisps of smoke are visible the oil is heated enough.
  4. Once oil has warmed place steak in pan. If the pan is heated enough there should be sizzling.
  5. Cook steak for one minute before turning.
  6. After one minute has passed, turn steak and cook for one more minute.
  7. Now that the steak has cooked for one minute each side, add the butter, minced garlic, and thyme to the pan.
  8. Continue to cook steak, turning every minute. Occasionally tilt the pan towards yourself and use a spoon to baste the steak with the melted butter.
  9. Once steak has finished cooking (About 5 minutes for rare, 7 minutes for medium, 10 minutes for well done, but it will depend on the size of the steak) remove from heat.
  10. Pour remaining butter on steak and let rest for two minutes.
  11. Enjoy!
