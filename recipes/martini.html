Classic Dry Martini

Description

This classic drink works in any setting and exudes style.

Ingredients

  • 1x Bottle of Gin
  • 1x Bottle of Sweet Vermouth
  • 3x Olives

Steps

  1. Combine a few ounces of gin with zero vermouth in a cocktail shaker, with ice.
  2. Swirl the gin in the shaker until chilled. Dont shake! Bond's martini is wrong and possibly immoral.
  3. As you swirl, try to envision the rolling hills of rural Italy. This will provide the correct amount of vermouth for the cocktail.
  4. Strain gin into a martini glass.
  5. Garnish with olives on a toothpick. One olive or three olives is appropriate. Two olives is wrong and possibly immoral.
  6. Drink martini.
  7. Repeat steps 1-6.
